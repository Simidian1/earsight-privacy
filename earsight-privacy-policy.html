Earsight Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Privacy-First Commitment: Earsight is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do not store your audio files, do not retain conversation content beyond what ChatGPT handles, and process all audio anonymously with immediate deletion after analysis.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Earsight ("we," "our," or "the Service"), an advanced audio analysis assistant built as a Custom GPT on OpenAI's ChatGPT platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use Earsight for audio analysis, room acoustics simulation, gear identification, and related audio services.

By using Earsight, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversation Data: Messages, questions, and descriptions you share with Earsight through the ChatGPT interface
  • Audio Files: Audio files you upload for analysis (transcription, spectral analysis, room simulation, etc.)
  • Images: Photos of rooms, audio equipment, or setups you share for visual analysis
  • Room Dimensions: Physical measurements and descriptions of listening environments
  • Equipment Information: Details about your audio gear, systems, and configurations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Metadata: Audio file properties (duration, format, sample rate, file size)
  • Usage Statistics: API endpoint usage, analysis types requested (fully anonymized)
  • Timestamps: When analyses are performed (for temporary logging only)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Voice prints or speaker identification for tracking purposes
  • Personal identification from audio content
  • Location data beyond what you explicitly provide for room analysis
  • Contact information (unless you voluntarily provide it in conversation)
  • Payment information (handled by OpenAI, not by Earsight)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Audio Analysis: Processing audio files to provide transcription, spectral analysis, quality assessment, and anomaly detection
  • Room Acoustics: Simulating acoustic behavior based on dimensions and materials you provide
  • Equipment Recommendations: Identifying gear and suggesting upgrades based on your system and goals
  • Hearing Safety: Analyzing sound pressure levels and providing informational hearing risk assessments
  • Service Improvement: Using anonymized usage statistics to improve analysis accuracy and features

3.2 We Do NOT Use Your Information For

  • Training AI models (your audio is never used for model training)
  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Selling or sharing with third parties
  • Building user profiles or tracking across services
  • Any purpose not directly related to providing the Service

4.1 Automatic Data Deletion

Our systems are designed with automatic deletion mechanisms:

  • Audio files are deleted immediately after processing completes
  • Temporary visualizations and generated audio expire within 24 hours
  • Metadata logs are automatically purged after 7 days
  • No manual intervention required for data deletion

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OpenAI / ChatGPT

Earsight operates as a Custom GPT on OpenAI's ChatGPT platform. Your conversations with Earsight are subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy and data handling practices:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • ChatGPT Terms: https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use
  • Data Controls: You can manage your ChatGPT data through your OpenAI account settings
  • Conversation History: Controlled by your ChatGPT settings (can be disabled)
Important: While Earsight does not store your audio files, your text conversations with the GPT are handled by OpenAI according to their policies. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy practices if you have concerns about conversation data.

5.2 Audio Processing API

Earsight uses a specialized audio analysis API (Ultimate Listener Assistant API) for advanced processing. This API follows the same privacy-first principles:

  • No audio storage beyond processing time
  • Anonymized processing with no PII tracking
  • HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encrypted transmission
  • No sharing with external third parties
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant

5.3 Equipment Database

When you request gear specifications, Earsight may query publicly available equipment databases and manufacturer specifications. No personal data is transmitted in these queries.

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Secure Processing: Audio processing in isolated, secure environments
  • Access Controls: Strict API authentication and rate limiting
  • No Persistent Storage: Audio files never written to persistent storage
  • Temporary URLs: Generated content accessible only via secure, expiring URLs
  • Anonymization: No personally identifiable information attached to audio processing

6.2 Limitations

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data in transit over the internet or stored temporarily during processing. By using Earsight, you acknowledge these inherent risks.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Access

Since we do not store audio content or personal data beyond temporary processing, there is typically no data to access. However, you may request confirmation of our data retention practices by contacting us.

7.2 Data Deletion

You can opt out of the Service at any time by simply not using Earsight. There are no ongoing data collection activities beyond active use.

7.4 Cookie and Tracking Opt-Out

Earsight does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Any cookies used are managed by OpenAI/ChatGPT for platform functionality.
